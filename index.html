Burrito

A burrito (English: /bəˈriːtoʊ/, Spanish: [buˈrito] ( listen)) is a dish in Mexican and Tex-Mex cuisine that consists of a flour tortilla with various other ingredients. It is wrapped into a closed-ended cylinder that can be picked up, in contrast to a taco, where the tortilla is simply folded around the fillings.
